TEFL (Teaching English to Foreign Learners)

What is TEFL?

TEFL stands for Teaching English as a Foreign Language. It's a certification program that equips you with the skills and knowledge to teach English to non-native speakers, either online or abroad.

Benefits of TEFL Certification

Global job opportunities: With a TEFL certification, you can teach English in various countries and cultures.
Career advancement: TEFL certification can boost your resume and increase your chances of landing a teaching job.
Flexibility: You can choose to teach online or abroad, full-time or part-time.
There is no degree or academic qualification needed. This course prepares you and trains you for what is to come.

Types of TEFL Courses

120-Hour TEFL Course: A basic course that covers the fundamentals of teaching English as a foreign language.
180-Hour TEFL Course: A more advanced course that provides in-depth training and is often preferred by employers.
Level 5 TEFL Diploma: A higher-level course that leads to a diploma and is equivalent to a CELTA or Trinity CertTESOL certification.

Cost and Duration

The price of our TEFL course aranges from R3500 - R12500. Course duration varies from 4 weeks to several months.

Job Opportunities

Online Teaching- With a TEFL certification, you can teach English online and work with students from around the world.
Teaching Abroad- You can teach English in various countries, including Spain, Thailand, China, and more.
