Who is LEHC:

At Lenzay Hope Education Centre, we are dedicated to empowering you through comprehensive TEFL (Teaching English as a Foreign Language) training and support. Our mission is to equip you with the skills, confidence, and tools you need to teach English to non-native speakers whether from the comfort of your own home or across the globe which includes opportunities for travel. We offer a complete pathway to your TEFL career, including:
  • In-depth TEFL training
  • Interview preparation
  • Video guidance and teaching tips
  • Job placement opportunities
